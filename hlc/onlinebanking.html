Financial Process Exchange

Make online payments anytime, anywhere with FPX!

Financial Process Exchange (FPX) is an online payment gateway that allows you to make real time online payments using FPX participating banks' current or savings account.

It's simple and convenient!

You can now make online payments anytime and anywhere with FPX available from 1:00 am to 11:00 pm daily, seven days a week, 365 days a year*

* FPX service is also dependent on the Internet Banking's service availability at the participating banks.

Here's what you need to use FPX:

  • Own a current or savings account with the FPX participating banks
  • Registered for Internet Banking service with the FPX participating banks

It's easy to use!

Login to participating merchants' web page and enter payment details.

Select Hong Leong Bank as debiting bank via FPX

You will be directed to Hong Leong Bank Connect website and key in your User name and Password

Upon approval, transaction value will be debited from your Hong Leong Bank account

You will receive an email notification confirming on the transactions
