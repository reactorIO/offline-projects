BEFORE REGISTRATION, PLEASE ENSURE:

  1. You have registered or updated your current mobile number for TAC at any of our branches or ATMs.
  2. You'll also need these items when registering with:

    3. ATM/Debit Card:

    • ATM/Debit card number
    • ATM PIN
    • TAC (Transaction Authorisation Code)

    Credit Card:

    • Credit Card number
    • Credit Card ATM PIN
    • TAC (Transaction Authorisation Code)

    Fixed Deposit or Loan Account:

    • Account number
    • Temporary ID*
    • TAC (Transaction Authorisation Code)

    *Please visit any of our branches to request for a Temporary ID

  3. Once you have the items above, register via:

    4. Online Banking

    Go to www.hongleongconnect.my and click on "First Time User" to register.

    Once you've registered, you can use the same username and password to access your Online and Mobile Banking app.

Eligibility: - You must be 18 years or above. - Individual or joint account holder with an active ATM / Debit Card. - Principal card holder of Hong Leong Bank Credit Card. - Account holder of Hong Leong bank Fixed Deposit or Loan Account - Sole proprietor who has an active ATM/Debit Card.
